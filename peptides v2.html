What Are Peptides?

Peptides are short chains of amino acids, the building blocks of protein. They play key roles in communication, repair, regeneration, and many functions in the body. Think of them as precise signals that tell your cells exactly what to do.

Communication

Signal molecules that tell your body what to do.

Repair

Support tissue repair and recovery.

Performance

Help support strength, endurance and output.

Longevity

Promote overall health and cellular function.
